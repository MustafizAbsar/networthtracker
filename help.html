How can we help?

How is my Financial Health Score calculated?

Your score (0-100) is a dynamic metric generated by analyzing your savings rate, debt-to-income ratio, emergency fund completion, and investment diversification. An ideal score above 80 indicates you are consistently spending less than you earn and reducing high-interest debt.

Is NetWorth connected directly to my bank?

No. NetWorth operates as a manual ledger and tracking system. We do not require or ask for your bank login credentials, ensuring your data remains completely private and entirely in your control.

How do I handle assets that appreciate (like property)?

To reflect property appreciation or investment gains, use the "Add Transaction" feature and select the "Asset Update" type. Enter the positive amount it has grown by, and it will automatically adjust your Net Worth and show up in the "Explain My Net Worth" breakdown.
