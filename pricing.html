Yes. We use bank-level 256-bit encryption. NetWorth only stores the balances and transactions you input. We do not connect directly to your bank accounts, meaning you maintain total control over your data.

Absolutely. You can change your billing cycle at any time from your account settings. If you switch to yearly, your remaining monthly balance will be prorated.
